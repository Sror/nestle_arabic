عالم جديد

Your baby is now hopefully breastfeeding but will soon be ready to try milk and/or infant formula. Be sure to talk to your family doctor before making this change. They will be able to recommend an infant formula if you cannot breastfeed, or choose not to. They can also explain the best time and way to wean your baby.

Milk four times a day – Your Baby is off to a great start! But if you choose not to breastfeed and want to introduce milk or infant formula, or when you start weaning your baby, it's important to get your doctor's advice. Once you have established a certain timing and your baby is confident with this, meal times will be much easier!

Your baby will soon be 6 months old. And ready to take milk from a bottle (as long as the bottle is sterilised first, of course)! Your baby will continue the "four times a day" rhythm in after 6 months – but this next stage will also bring a lot of exciting changes, like the first taste of cereals without gluten, and the first purée!
