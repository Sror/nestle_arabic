NIDO® ONE PLUS is a specialized growing-up milk designed for your 1-3 year old child to help support overall growth, development and immunity.


Here’s what makes NIDO® ONE PLUS superior to any regular milk…

  • Adapted Protein content to provide your child with the right amount of protein from milk without overloading his growing yet immature kidneys.
  • Healthy fat profile with الأحماض الدهنية الأساسية for proper growth and development.
  • Fortified with الحديد to help prevent from iron-deficiency anemia and needed for your child’s normal brain development.
  • Fortified with Vitamins and minerals including Vitamins A, C, D, E, الحديد, الزنك and Selenium that help support your child’s immunity, overall growth and development.
  • Lactobacillus PROTECTUS®: Live probiotic cultures that help boost your child’s natural body defenses.
  • A natural blend of dietary prebiotic fibers developed by Nestlé that contributes your child’s healthy digestive system.
