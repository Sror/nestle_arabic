  • تطور الامكانيات الجسدية والفكرية لولدك بين الثلاث و الأربع سنوات
    • بلغ طفلك الثلاث سنوات، وأصبح الآن أكثر استقلالية، يتمكن تقريبا ً من الاعتناء بنفسه ويعبر ويطلب ما يريده بوضوحٍ أكبر. خياله المليء بالسحر سيسيطر على تصرفاته خلال السنة القادمة. إليك بعض حركات ومؤشرات التطور التي تظهر في الأولاد ما بين الثلاث الأربع سنوات.
  • مؤشرات تطور الحركات الجسدية:
    • Your preschooler is in constant movement all time as he is trying to convey the messages he cannot completely explain using words yet. His movements are quite agile now; he can go up and down the stairs without support, jump and stand on one foot up to five seconds. A 3 year old can ride a tricycle with ease and can kick and throw the ball easily and catch a bouncing ball most of the time. They love games such as tag, catch or playing ball with other children.
      Tip: your supervision remains essential to prevent some injuries, yet allow a lot of time for your child to play alone.
      Your 3 year old is slowly gaining control over his muscles and this will enable him to concentrate on using his hands and fingers more precisely. You probably have noticed that he is holding a crayon more like an adult and can now copy some shapes and capital letters. He can draw persons with a few body parts and use the safety scissors. He now dress or undress with little help and become more and more independent in self-care.
      Tip: Encourage your child to develop their manual abilities by engaging in activities such as building blocks and zipping, unzipping, buttoning and unbuttoning cloths.
  • مؤشرات تطور اللغة والمعرفة:
    • الولد البالغ من العمر ثلاث سنوات يمكنه تأليف جمل من 5 إلى 6 كلمات، وبما يكفي من الوضوح للفهم. وعلامة بارزة تظهر بين الثلاث سنوات والنصف والسن الرابع هي استخدامه لكلمة "نحن" وبقولها فأنه يفهم موقفه من الآخرين ويشق طريقه في المجتمع. يحب تأليف القصص من خياله الخاص، والمشاركة في اللعب الخيالي (أخذ دور الطبيب أو الأم أو المعلم). يسمي أغلب الألوان بشكلٍ صحيح، ويفهم مبدأ العد وقد يتمكن من العد قليلاً، ويفهم أيضاً فكرة التشابه أو الاختلاف. الإدراك بالوقت يصبح أكثر وضوحاً الآن.
  • مؤشرات التطور الاجتماعي والعاطفي:
    • A 3 year old shows a great interest in playing and interacting with other children, and would not mind you leaving him at preschool.
      Starting 3 years of age, your child’s imagination will guide his actions. This is the time where your child is interested in details and starts to ask those famous “why?”, “where?”, “how?” questions. His vocabulary has progressed and enables him to express his adventures or fairy tales of his own creation with attention to details: “I killed the wolf with my sword!” They show a bigger interest in books that help in inventing their own stories; almost all children like to be told stories, so try to make this a ritual that can stay with them beyond childhood. His vivid imagination makes it difficult for him to distinguish between fantasy and reality.
      Acting and dressing up for the role becomes his favorite hobby. Little girls love to dress-up their dolls, while little boys love to drive their trucks and miniature cars and trains. Both of them like construction toys, big puzzles, coloring books and the outside play swings and slides. Tip: Be sure to take all the little questions that your young one asks seriously; as this will feed his curiosity and knowledge and will help him to think more clearly.
  • بين المنزل والمدرسة:
    • في سنوات المدرسة الأولى، يتعلم الولد قواعد الإنضباط ويتغذى خياله وتتطور مهاراته الذهنية والإجتماعية. سينمو طفلك ويتعلم الكثيرعن طريق اللعب مع الأطفال الآخرين من عمره، ويتمكن بالتعبير عن نفسه بوضوح للبالغين الذي تعرف عليهم جديداً في المدرسة ويشق شيئاً فشيئاً طريقه نحو الإستقلالية. من خلال اللعب، سوف يتعلم ولدك التركيز والسيطرة على نفسه وهذا يعني أنه عليه للاستماع وفهم وتننفيذ ما يقال له في الصف. ليس من السهل في البداية، ولكن سوف تكونين بانتظاره في المنزل لتشجيعه ودعمه.
  • التطورات المتعلقة بكيفية تناول الأولاد الصغار من 3 إلى 4 سنوات للطعام
    • Your young child has gained muscular control of his hands and is now able to concentrate in order to poor water from a bottle into a cup without over pouring or even serving himself without spilling. At this age, young children develop Spatial awareness, which will make them take great care while feeding themselves and when using utensils so they don’t spill. They can use a fork but might still hold it with their fist. Being at preschool children will adapt to eating in group lunches along with their friends and will learn quite a lot from them.
      At this age, children might reject certain foods and have a favorite menu that they would like to stick to, but keep on offering a variety of healthy foods and always encourage young children to try new foods.
      Encourage him to be More and more independent
      Your child is growing and so are his responsibilities! A preschooler is constantly busy experimenting, talking, singing and dancing, whether at home or in pre-school. His agenda is packed with activities that will help him grow smarter and stronger each day. Reminding our busy bees to take a break, wash their hands and enjoy a healthy and yummy meal or snack is something we should do 4 to 5 times a day but make sure to encourage independence by:
      •    Encourage a young child to wash his hands alone, if dedicated sinks are available, or if provided a stool to       stand on
      •    Do not assist in feeding, but expect a little spilling between their mouth and plate occasionally
      •    Encourage him to sit and eat alone on the table
      •    If he still holds the spoon with his fists, very soon around 4 he will be able to manipulate kitchen utensils like       you do; so make sure to arrange for at least one family meal daily to allow him to learn from you
      •    Encourage him to brush his teeth alone and change his cloths if necessary after meals.

    • عندما يبلغ الأولاد الرابعة من العمر، يتحسن التنسيق والتوازن الجسدي لديهم، فيصبح الولد دقيق بألعاب الكرة ويتعامل أفضل بكثير بالألعاب التي تحتاج إلى الصبر والتركيز مثل الألغاز. ولكن قبل كل شيء، ما ستحبين به هو قدرته على التواصل معك والرعاية بنفسه، مثل اللباس، واستخدام الحمام والعديد من الوظائف اليومية الأخرى.
  • مؤشرات تطور الحركات الجسدية:
    • A young 4 to 5 years old is physically stronger and can engage a whole lot of activities like swinging alone, or skipping a rope maybe. By 5 years of age, the child’s physical activity increases and becomes more daring both at home and at school. He will test his physical limits to gain admiration and that of the people around him.
      Tip: Encourage him to commit in a sports of his choice. Always watch over him in some situations like swimming or crossing the street for example. By age four, your young child can use his hands to the fullest now that his coordination is fully developed. He enjoys building complex structures with many blocks, and can now draw a person with an entire body, geometric patterns, print some letters, paint, model clay, cut and paste. At this age, children enjoy engaging in Arts and Crafts as their self-esteem grows along with their new creations.
      By the time he turns five, your child will be able to brush his teeth, get dressed alone and maybe able to lace his own shoes.
      Tip: Let your child take the direction he enjoys most by engaging him in different activities, whether it is Sports, Arts and Crafts or any other activity.
  • مؤشرات تطور اللغة والمعرفة:
    • يتمكن الأولاد بعد السن الرابع من إتلاء حكايات اطول وتذكر قصص رُوت إليهم في الماضي، باستطاعتهم تسمية الألوان والأشكال بشكل صحيح ، وفهم مبدأ العد، والأبجدية و مضدات الحجم (طويل القامة مقابل قصير القامة). ولدك الآن يستخدم الصفات، الظروف، ويدرك الوقت بشكلٍ أفضل كما أنه يتفهم ما هو الأمس واليوم وغدا. ويسأل: "هل حان الوقت ل...؟" يستخدم الآن صيغة المستقبل والشرط: "إذا ...فسوف.....". ولازل الخيال يسيطر على تصرفاته.
  • مؤشرات التطور الاجتماعي والعاطفي:
    • كما قبل، يحب الولد اللعب عن طريق الإدعاء وتقليد بعض الأشخاص ولكنه الآن يتعلم أن يميز الواقع من الخيال. ويتميز الأولاد بعد السن الرابع بمرادهم لإرضاء الأصدقاء ويبحثون إلى أن يكونوا مثلهم. أوقاتهم المفضلة تبقى أوقات الغناء والرقص وهم أقل خجل من عندما كانوا أصغر سنا. هم أكثر فضولية، ودية وثرثرة. هم حساسون لمشاعر الآخرين. في الوقت عينه، قد تكون الفتيات الصغيرات تكن بعض الإعجاب لوالدها والصبية الصغار لأمهاتهم وهذا الإعجاب سوف يختفي مع مرور الوقت من تلقاء نفسه طالما أن الآباء لا يعيرون الموضوع اهتماما كبيراُ.
  • الاستعداد للمدرسة
    • Want to get your child ready for school? Here are some tips:
      •    Always encourage your child to tell you about his day, friends and activities
      •    Have a quiet reading time together each day and ask your child questions about the stories
      •    Encourage your child to talk in full sentences
      •    Listen to and treat your child with respect. Insist that others do so as well
      •    Encourage him to be kind to others and apologize after hurting someone’s feelings
      •    Help your child express his feelings
      •    Give your child the chance to play with others often
      •    Consider enrolling your child in preschool at the age of three or earlier if necessary.
  • من 4 إلى 5 سنة من العمر
    • Four year olds gain a lot of coordination skills and a great ability to use their hands. So, young children can hold a fork or a spoon like an adult. Your little one is eager to learn everything so instruct him on how to use a knife and encourage him to use it under your supervision. They are now clean and are able to brush their teeth alone, use the toilet alone and change their cloths if asked to. Good time to Teach Table Manners
      After the age of four, children are generally good company but do not hesitate to encourage and reward good table manners such as not talking with the mouth full, using a napkin and not sleeves to wipe his mouth, and not reaching to another person’s plate. Do not miss the opportunity to gather around a family meal and model all the necessary etiquette; they will learn faster by imitating you. Involve your child in setting the table and in the meal preparation.

How do you help your child to

Play creatively

and think?


More
  • Find a balance in his possibilities for play: indoor, outdoor and group play and arrange for toys and involve in activities that include:
    •    Natural Materials: playing with clay and dough, sand, stones and leaves,
    •    Basic Engineering: Building blocks and fitting toys: threading toys and puzzles
    •    Physical play: Climbing, Swinging, running, throwing, catching and kicking, push and pull toys as well as ride-on       toys
    •    Imaginative play: Miniature houses, Domestic play such as helping in house chores, dressing up and playing with       dolls and soft toys
    •    Looking and Listening: read story books and listen to music, limit TV as much as possible
    •    Creativity: Painting and drawing, cutting and sticking
    -     Read books together and engage in conversation by discussing them
    -     Keep some time for peaceful alone time to encourage amazing discoveries
    -     Organize his toys, in a play space of his own that is safe enough and where he can do whatever       he pleases without assistance or surveillance will help concentrate creative play.
    -     Providing too much support and demonstrations would rob his imagination, just be there when he       needs you and offer casual suggestions.
    -     Help him concentrate on puzzles or fitting toys by sitting with him, talk, support and encourage       him. Each time he will be able to go for longer until he completes the task. Completing it will give       him satisfaction and your role is to congratulate him.
